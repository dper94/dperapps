Privacy Policy — Quiet Mode

Last Updated: 02/05/2026

Quiet Mode ("we", "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

  • No registration or login: We do not request personal information from users.
  • Analytics data: We use Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience. This includes:
    • App interactions (features used, events within the app).
    • Approximate location (city/region level, not precise GPS).
    • Device information (model, operating system version, language settings).
    • Important: This data is used solely for internal analysis to improve the app. We do not share this information with third parties.
  • Subscriptions: To enable premium features, we use Google Play / App Store, which process payments and handle subscription information.
  • Accessibility and notification services:
    • We use the accessibility service to detect which apps are active and enable focus sessions.
    • We use the notification service to identify distracting notifications and block them when appropriate.
    • Important: This information is not stored or shared with third parties; it is only used for the app's functionality within the device.

2. Use of information

  • The information collected is used solely to enable Quiet Mode's functionality, such as blocking apps and notifications and managing focus sessions.
  • Analytics data collected through Firebase is used exclusively for internal purposes to understand how users interact with the app and improve its features and performance.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

3. Security

  • All subscription purchases are managed by Google Play / App Store, which apply industry-standard security measures.
  • Analytics data collected through Firebase Analytics is transmitted securely and stored in accordance with Google's privacy and security standards.
  • Information obtained through accessibility and notification services remains on the device.

4. Your data rights

  • Data deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of all data associated with your use of the app.
  • To request data deletion, please contact us at the email address provided below. We will process your request and delete all your data from our systems.
  • Please note that data related to subscription purchases is managed by Google Play / App Store and must be handled according to their respective policies.

5. Changes to the policy

  • We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
